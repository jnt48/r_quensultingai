Frequently Asked

Quick Answers

Our AI receptionist can answer all of these questions — and more — during your call.

What are your clinic timings? ▼

We are open Monday through Saturday, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Do you accept walk-in patients? ▼

Yes, we do accept walk-in patients! However, booking an appointment in advance is highly recommended to avoid waiting, especially during peak hours.

What is the consultation fee? ▼

Our general dental consultation fee is ₹500. Fees for specialized treatments vary — please ask our receptionist for a detailed quote for your specific service.

Where is the clinic located? ▼

We are conveniently located in Pune, Maharashtra. Please contact our AI receptionist for the exact address and directions tailored to your location.

Do you provide emergency appointments? ▼

Yes! We accommodate emergency dental appointments during our working hours (Mon–Sat, 9 AM–6 PM). Please call our AI receptionist immediately for urgent assistance.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept Cash, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and all major UPI platforms (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.).
